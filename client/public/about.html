About Us

Formed in September 2018, just for the purpose of this CodeClan JavaScript project, RASR is made up of 4 team members:
Ricky, Anne, Shannon, and Robert.
The BBC Educational App entitled Landmarks is the first app they have worked collaboratively on.
It currently only contains man-made landmarks 🗿 🏛️ ⛩️ 🕌 🗽, and even it is not an exhaustive list of landmarks.

Curriculum Link: Social Studies

I can learn how to locate, explore and link features and places locally and further afield.
To extend my mental map and sense of place, I can interpret information from different types of maps and am beginning to locate key features within Scotland, UK, Europe or the wider world. SOC 2-14a

🤔 Future Updates 🤔

Future updates that are being considered include:

  1. Natural Landmarks 🏞️ - including: The Grand Canyon, The Great Barrier Reef, and Niagara Falls.
  2. Audio buttons 🎧 - to hear the landmark information read aloud.
  3. Interactive Quizzes ✅ - linked to landmarks and landmark information.
  4. Request a New Landmark Feature ✉️ - to receive input from users to add landmarks to the app.
